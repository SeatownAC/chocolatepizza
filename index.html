For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a sauvepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissovle. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissovled and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Wisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait unitl the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, poor liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  1. 1 1/2 cups milk
  2. 1/2 cup mascarpone
  3. 1/2 tsp salt
  4. 1 lb black mission figs
  5. 1/2cup brown sugar
  6. 2-4 tbsp water
  7. 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  8. 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  9. 2 egg yolks
  10. 1 lemon, juiced
  11. 2 tbsp butter
  12. 1 cup honey roasted pecans, chopped
